# Business Rules Reference

## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive business rules that govern the Ban Sai Yai Savings Group Financial Accounting System. These rules ensure regulatory compliance, financial integrity, and operational consistency.

## Member Management Rules

### Registration Requirements
1. **Age Requirement**: Members must be at least 18 years old
2. **Identification**: Valid government-issued ID card required
3. **Address Verification**: Residential address must be verified
4. **Photo Documentation**: Recent passport-sized photo required
5. **Duplicate Prevention**: No duplicate members with same ID card
6. **Initial Deposit**: Minimum initial share capital required

### Member Eligibility
1. **Membership Tenure**: Minimum 6 months for loan eligibility
2. **Active Status**: Member must be in good standing
3. **Share Capital**: Minimum share capital maintenance required
4. **Attendance**: Regular meeting attendance may be required
5. **Background Check**: No history of loan defaults

## Savings Account Rules

### Deposit Rules
1. **Minimum Deposit**: THB 100 minimum per deposit
2. **Share Capital**: Minimum THB 1,000 share capital
3. **Deposit Frequency**: No limit on deposit frequency
4. **Verification**: All deposits require proper documentation
5. **Source of Funds**: Must disclose source for large deposits

### Withdrawal Rules
1. **Notice Period**: 7-day notice required for withdrawals
2. **Minimum Balance**: Maintain minimum share capital
3. **Withdrawal Limits**: Maximum withdrawal amount per transaction
4. **Approval**: Large withdrawals may require committee approval
5. **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days for processing

### Interest Calculation
1. **Interest Rate**: As determined by board of directors
2. **Calculation Method**: Daily balance method
3. **Compounding**: Monthly compounding
4. **Posting**: Interest posted monthly
5. **Tax Withholding**: Tax withheld at source as required

## Loan Management Rules

### Loan Eligibility
1. **Membership Period**: Minimum 6 months as active member
2. **Credit History**: No previous loan defaults
3. **Share Ratio**: Loan amount cannot exceed 5x share capital
4. **Income Verification**: Demonstrated repayment capacity
5. **Existing Loans**: No more than 2 active loans

### Loan Types and Limits
1. **Personal Loan**: Maximum THB 50,000, 24 months term
2. **Business Loan**: Maximum THB 200,000, 36 months term
3. **Emergency Loan**: Maximum THB 20,000, 12 months term
4. **Education Loan**: Maximum THB 100,000, 48 months term
5. **Housing Loan**: Maximum THB 500,000, 120 months term

### Interest Rates
1. **Base Rate**: As determined by board
2. **Risk Premium**: Additional rate based on loan type
3. **Maximum Rate**: Regulatory maximum rate cap
4. **Calculation Method**: Reducing balance method
5. **Payment Frequency**: Monthly payments required

### Collateral Requirements
1. **Collateral Value**: Minimum 120% of loan value
2. **Acceptable Collateral**: Property, vehicles, savings deposits
3. **Valuation**: Professional valuation required
4. **Documentation**: Proper ownership documentation
5. **Insurance**: Collateral must be insured

### Guarantor Requirements
1. **Number of Guarantors**: Minimum 2 for loans > THB 100,000
2. **Guarantor Eligibility**: Must be active member for 1 year
3. **Guarantor Capacity**: Cannot guarantee more than 3 active loans
4. **Guarantor Limits**: Maximum guarantee amount = share capital x 2
5. **Joint Liability**: Guarantors have joint and several liability

## Approval Workflow Rules

### Authority Levels
1. **Loan Officer**: Can process loan applications up to THB 50,000
2. **Secretary**: Can review and recommend loans up to THB 200,000
3. **President**: Final approval for all loans
4. **Board Approval**: Required for loans > THB 500,000
5. **Emergency Authority**: Special authority for emergency loans

### Approval Process
1. **Application Review**: Complete application verification
2. **Credit Assessment**: Credit score and capacity evaluation
3. **Collateral Verification**: Collateral ownership and value verification
4. **Guarantor Check**: Guarantor eligibility verification
5. **Approval Decision**: Final approval with proper documentation

### Rejection Rules
1. **Clear Reasons**: Specific rejection reasons must be provided
2. **Reapplication Period**: 3-month waiting period for rejected applications
3. **Documentation**: Rejection must be properly documented
4. **Communication**: Member must be notified of rejection reasons
5. **Appeal Process**: Right to appeal within 30 days

## Payment Processing Rules

### Payment Allocation
1. **Priority Order**: Interest first, then principal
2. **Late Payment**: Penalty charges apply after grace period
3. **Prepayment**: Early repayment allowed without penalty
4. **Partial Payments**: Accepted but may extend loan term
5. **Payment Methods**: Cash, bank transfer, mobile money accepted

### Penalty Calculation
1. **Grace Period**: 5-day grace period for monthly payments
2. **Penalty Rate**: 5% per annum on overdue amount
3. **Calculation Method**: Daily calculation of penalties
4. **Maximum Penalty**: Capped at 25% of overdue amount
5. **Waiver Authority**: President can waive penalties in exceptional cases

### Receipt Generation
1. **Automatic Generation**: Receipts generated for all payments
2. **Receipt Number**: Unique sequential numbering
3. **Receipt Details**: Complete payment breakdown included
4. **Digital Copy**: PDF copy sent to member
5. **Retention**: Receipts retained for 7 years

## Financial Reporting Rules

### Balance Sheet Requirements
1. **Asset Classification**: Current and non-current assets
2. **Liability Classification**: Current and long-term liabilities
3. **Equity Presentation**: Share capital and retained earnings
4. **Balancing**: Assets must equal liabilities plus equity
5. **Currency**: All amounts in Thai Baht (THB)

### Profit & Loss Requirements
1. **Revenue Recognition**: Interest income recognized when earned
2. **Expense Recognition**: Expenses recognized when incurred
3. **Matching Principle**: Revenues matched with related expenses
4. **Period Comparison**: Year-over-year comparisons required
5. **Disclosure Requirements**: All material items disclosed

### Trial Balance Rules
1. **Balancing**: Debits must equal credits
2. **Account Classification**: Proper classification of all accounts
3. **Adjustment Entries**: All adjustments properly documented
4. **Review Process**: Trial balance must be reviewed by auditor
5. **Error Correction**: Errors corrected promptly and documented

## Dividend Calculation Rules

### Profit Determination
1. **Net Profit**: Calculated after all expenses and taxes
2. **Reserve Requirements**: Legal reserves maintained as required
3. **Retained Earnings**: Portion of profit retained for growth
4. **Distributable Profit**: Profit available for dividend distribution
5. **Audit Verification**: Profit verified by independent auditor

### Dividend Allocation
1. **Share Dividend**: Based on share capital contribution
2. **Deposit Dividend**: Based on average savings balance
3. **Interest Refund**: Portion of interest refunded to members
4. **Tax Withholding**: Dividend tax withheld at source
5. **Payment Method**: Dividends credited to savings accounts

### Distribution Rules
1. **Eligibility**: Members must be active for full year
2. **Calculation Period**: Annual calculation on fiscal year-end
3. **Declaration Date**: Dividends declared within 3 months of year-end
4. **Payment Date**: Dividends paid within 30 days of declaration
5. **Documentation**: Complete dividend statements provided

## Accounting Rules

### Double-Entry Bookkeeping
1. **Debit/Credit**: Every transaction has equal debit and credit
2. **Account Classification**: Proper classification of all accounts
3. **Balancing**: Trial balance must always balance
4. **Audit Trail**: Complete audit trail for all transactions
5. **Period Closing**: Proper month-end and year-end procedures

### Chart of Accounts
1. **Account Structure**: Hierarchical account structure
2. **Account Codes**: Unique account codes for each account
3. **Account Types**: Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses
4. **Inactive Accounts**: Inactive accounts clearly marked
5. **Changes**: Chart of accounts changes properly documented

### Journal Entry Rules
1. **Entry Requirements**: Date, description, amount, accounts required
2. **Approval Process**: Journal entries require approval before posting
3. **Documentation**: Supporting documentation required for all entries
4. **Corrections**: Correction entries properly documented and authorized
5. **Security**: Proper segregation of duties for journal entries

## Compliance and Regulatory Rules

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
1. **Transaction Monitoring**: Large transactions monitored
2. **Suspicious Activity**: Suspicious transactions reported
3. **Customer Due Diligence**: Proper customer verification
4. **Record Keeping**: Transaction records retained for 5 years
5. **Training**: Regular AML training for staff

### Data Protection
1. **Member Privacy**: Member information kept confidential
2. **Data Security**: Proper security measures for data protection
3. **Access Control**: Limited access to sensitive information
4. **Data Retention**: Data retained as per legal requirements
5. **Breach Notification**: Data breaches reported promptly

### Financial Regulations
1. **Licensing**: Proper licensing from financial authorities
2. **Reporting Requirements**: Regular reporting to regulators
3. **Capital Requirements**: Minimum capital requirements maintained
4. **Audit Requirements**: Annual audit by certified auditor
5. **Compliance Training**: Regular compliance training for staff

## Risk Management Rules

### Credit Risk
1. **Credit Scoring**: Standardized credit scoring system
2. **Risk Assessment**: Regular risk assessment of loan portfolio
3. **Concentration Limits**: Limits on concentration of loans
4. **Provisioning**: Adequate provisions for bad loans
5. **Monitoring**: Regular monitoring of loan performance

### Operational Risk
1. **Internal Controls**: Proper internal controls implemented
2. **Segregation of Duties**: Proper segregation of critical functions
3. **Backup Systems**: Regular backup of critical data
4. **Disaster Recovery**: Disaster recovery plan in place
5. **Testing**: Regular testing of systems and procedures

### Market Risk
1. **Interest Rate Risk**: Monitoring of interest rate risk
2. **Liquidity Risk**: Adequate liquidity maintained
3. **Currency Risk**: Foreign exchange risk managed
4. **Investment Guidelines**: Investment guidelines followed
5. **Stress Testing**: Regular stress testing performed

## Enforcement and Penalties

### Rule Violations
1. **Warning**: Written warning for first violation
2. **Suspension**: Suspension of privileges for repeated violations
3. **Termination**: Membership termination for serious violations
4. **Legal Action**: Legal action for fraudulent activities
5. **Reporting**: Serious violations reported to authorities

### Appeals Process
1. **Appeal Period**: 30 days to appeal decisions
2. **Appeal Committee**: Independent committee for appeals
3. **Evidence**: Member can submit evidence supporting appeal
4. **Decision Time**: Appeals decided within 15 days
5. **Final Decision**: Appeal committee decision is final

## Rule Updates and Amendments

### Rule Changes
1. **Board Approval**: All rule changes require board approval
2. **Member Notification**: Members notified of rule changes
3. **Effective Date**: Clear effective date for rule changes
4. **Documentation**: All changes properly documented
5. **Training**: Staff trained on new rules

### Review Process
1. **Annual Review**: Annual review of all business rules
2. **Regulatory Updates**: Rules updated based on regulatory changes
3. **Best Practices**: Incorporation of industry best practices
4. **Member Feedback**: Member feedback considered in rule updates
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Continuous improvement of rules and procedures
